Who We Are

This Digital Learning Platform was created under the Smart India Hackathon to empower rural school students in Nabha. Our goal is to provide offline-first, interactive, and localized digital education that bridges the gap between rural and urban learning.

Our Mission Journey

  • 2025

    Project launched under Smart India Hackathon with focus on Nabha schools.

  • Phase 1

    Designed offline-first lessons and digital literacy modules in local languages.

  • Phase 2

    Teacher dashboard and student progress tracking introduced.

  • Future

    Scale to other rural areas and integrate adaptive learning technology.
